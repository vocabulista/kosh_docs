## C-SALT APIs for Sanskrit Dictionaries

The C-SALT APIs for Sanskrit Dictionaries provide access to the most important Sanskrit dictionaries hosted by the University of Cologne. The dictionaries have been encoded in TEI-P5 and you can download them from [this repository](https://github.com/cceh/c-salt_sanskrit_data).
